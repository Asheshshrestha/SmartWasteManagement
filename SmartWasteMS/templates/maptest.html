    Features

    Features

    Dynamic Routing

    Our solution uses real-time and historical data to optimize waste collection schedules and routes. Predictive analytics enables users to make decisions ahead of time and offers insight into waste bin locations.

    Cost Reduction

    Our solution reduces waste collection frequency dramatically which enables you to save on fuel, labor, and fleet maintenance costs. Altogether our products can reduce your operational costs by up to 80%.

    Improved cleaness

    In densely populated areas, rapid waste generation often leads to overflowing waste bins and littered streets. Our solution enables custodians to monitor fill-levels throughout the day and take action on notifications regarding overflowing bins.

    CO2 Reduction

    Current waste collection methods are pollution heavy as it is well known that waste trucks get abysmal fuel efficiency. Our solution offers you the means to have fewer trucks on the road for less time, which means less carbon emissions, less noise, less traffic, and less wear and tear on roads.
